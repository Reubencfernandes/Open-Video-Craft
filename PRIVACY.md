# Privacy Policy for Open Video Craft

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Open Video Craft is a screen recorder and video editor designed to perform its core recording, editing, subtitle processing, project storage, and export work locally on your Mac. You do not need to create an Open Video Craft account.

## Data we do not collect

Open Video Craft does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, or an Open Video Craft telemetry service. We do not sell personal information.

## Data stored on your Mac

Recordings, imported media, project files, subtitles, exports, settings, and recovery data are stored locally in locations you choose or in the app's local storage. Optional third-party API keys are stored locally and are protected with the macOS keychain-backed secure storage when it is available.

## Optional third-party services

The app can connect to third-party AI services only when you explicitly configure and use those features:

- Google Gemini features may send the prompt and the video, audio, transcript, or other project content needed to complete your request to Google.
- Cohere cloud transcription may send extracted audio and the API credential needed to authenticate the request to Cohere.
- Claude Code integration communicates with the locally installed Claude Code tool and is subject to the settings and terms of that tool.

These transfers are used only to provide the feature you requested. Open Video Craft does not use them for advertising or tracking. The third-party provider processes and retains the information under its own terms and privacy policy. You can use the core recording and editing features without enabling these integrations.

## macOS permissions

Open Video Craft requests Screen & System Audio Recording permission to capture your screen. Camera and Microphone permissions are requested only when you enable those inputs. File access is requested when you choose project, import, or export locations. These permissions are used to provide the corresponding app features.

## Data retention and deletion

You control locally stored projects and exports and can delete them using Finder. Removing a saved third-party credential from the app deletes its local saved copy. Data sent to an optional third-party service is governed by that provider's retention and deletion practices.

## Children's privacy

Open Video Craft is a general-purpose creative tool and is not directed to children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change, this policy will be updated and the revision date above will change.
